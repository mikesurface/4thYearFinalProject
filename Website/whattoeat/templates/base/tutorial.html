Nutrients and Meal Requirements

WhatToEat considers the following 8 nutrients:

  1. Calories (in kcals)
  2. Protein (in grams)
  3. Total Carbohydrate (in grams)
  4. Total Fat (in grams)
  5. Saturated Fat (in grams)
  6. Salt (in grams)
  7. Sugar (in grams)
  8. Fibre (in grams)

Generating a Meal

Adding an Ingredient

A search feature is provided to find and add ingredients to meals. Typically, there will be several portions available for an ingredient. Choose whichever one suits you.

Portion Fixing

You can fix the portion of an ingredient in the meal generator. The means only an exact multiple of the provided serving may be included within the ingredient. If for example you provide "100g of Chicken" as a fixed portion, valid meals may include 0g,100g,200g,etc of chicken. Otherwise any amount of chicken may be included.

Restricting Ingredients

You can restrict ingredients by setting a restriction on the ingredient with a threshold value. The restricts the quantity of the ingredient which may be allowed in a generated meal. The threshold must be specified in grams or millilitres. Which one to use is determined by the metric value for the serving size (given in brackets after the sercing size).
