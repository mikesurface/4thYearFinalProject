Welcome to WhatToEat

WhatToEat is an application to support the construction of nutritionally sound meals, personalised to fit your goals. WhatToEat's main feature is the Meal Generator. This takes in your nutritional requirements and a set of ingredients you have chosen and trys to construct a meal from the ingredients which satisfies the requirements.

WhatToEat can support you in achieving one of three possible goals:

  1. Losing Weight
  2. Gaining Weight
  3. Maintaining Your Current Weight

To start, sign up for an account or log in if you are already registered.
